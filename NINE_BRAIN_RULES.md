# Verum Omnis — Nine-Brain Rules & Constitution Reference

## 1. The Nine-Brain Architecture

| Brain | Core Function | Voting Status | Key Operational Rules |
|-------|--------------|---------------|----------------------|
| B1 | Contradiction Detection | **VOTING** | 16 contradiction types (5 legacy + 11 AllFuels). Must agree with 2+ other brains. Multi-pass scanning. |
| B2 | Document Forensics | **VOTING** | SHA-512, PDF/A-3B, watermark verification. Tamper score 0.000 (verified) to 1.000 (fully compromised). Must comply with Daubert, ISO 27001, GDPR. |
| B3 | Communications Integrity | **VOTING** | Every message sealed, audited, logged. Redactions are immutable. Emergency access requires dual human-AI unlock. |
| B4 | Behavioral Analysis | **VOTING** | LIWC++ algorithms. Per-person liability scorecard (0-10). Voice tone analysis for threats, stress, deception. |
| B5 | Timeline Reconstruction | **VOTING** | Identifies missing/deleted entries. ISP logs, GPS, device logs. 2+ year gap = consciousness of guilt flag. |
| B6 | Financial Analysis | **VOTING** | Every transaction reconciled against ledgers and tax codes. Simulates fraud patterns. Auto-generates tax returns. Rent tracing (v5.2.8). |
| B7 | Legal Mapping | **VOTING** | Jurisdiction-specific (UAE, SA, US, EU, UN). Auto-maps statutes, precedents, treaties. SAFLII/PACER/BAILII online retrieval (v5.2.8). |
| B8 | Audio Forensics | **VOTING** | Whisper.cpp on-device transcription. Speaker diarization. Synthetic audio detection. |
| B9 | R&D / Red Team | **NON-VOTING** | Cannot issue verdicts. Trains and calibrates all other brains via simulation. Adversarial red-team testing. v5.2.8 upgrade: votes ONLY on online judicial record authentication. |

**Consensus Rule:** A contradiction is accepted only when B1 flags it AND at least 2 other brains confirm. If quorum is not met, output is "INSUFFICIENT" or "INDETERMINATE_DUE_TO_CONCEALMENT." B9 does not count toward the 3-brain quorum.

**Voting vs Non-Voting:** B1-B8 can each vote on findings. B9 cannot. B9's role is to train, validate, and red-team — to find what the other brains missed. In v5.2.8, B9 gained a limited voting exception: it may vote ONLY on the authentication of online judicial records retrieved by B7.

## 3. Brain-Specific Constitutional Rules

### B1 — Contradiction Brain
- Must detect all 16 contradiction types (5 legacy: SWORN_VS_DOCUMENT, DIRECT_NEGATION, TEMPORAL_SHIFT, ROLE_INCONSISTENCY, ACTION_VS_WORDS; 11 AllFuels: JUDICIAL_VS_DOCUMENTARY, TEMPORAL_CONTRADICTION, CONSCIOUSNESS_OF_GUILT, PERJURY_BY_TIMELINE, PATTERN_OF_RACKETEERING, REGULATORY_CAPTURE, SHAM_TRANSACTION, FRAUD_ON_THE_COURT, CORPORATE_VEIL_ABUSE, TACIT_LEASE_VIOLATION, POST_EXPIRY_ENFORCEMENT)
- Multi-pass scanning: first pass for direct contradictions, second pass for implied/temporal, third pass for cross-document judicial
- Every contradiction must be anchored to: person, page/line, applicable statute

### B2 — Document Brain
- Tamper score: 0.000 = fully verified, 1.000 = fully compromised
- Must verify: SHA-512 hash, PDF/A-3B compliance, watermark presence, metadata consistency
- Standards: Daubert, ISO 27001, GDPR

### B3 — Communications Brain
- Every message sealed with individual hash
- Redactions are immutable (cannot be altered once applied)
- Emergency access requires dual human-AI unlock
- Deleted message detection via sequence gap analysis

### B4 — Behavioral Brain
- LIWC++ algorithms for linguistic pattern detection
- Per-person liability scorecard: 0 (no concern) to 10 (severe manipulation)
- Voice tone analysis: threat detection, stress markers, deception indicators
- Gaslighting pattern recognition: fact denial, contradiction cycling, reality revision

### B5 — Timeline Brain
- Identifies missing or deleted timeline entries
- Cross-references ISP logs, GPS data, device logs
- 2+ year gap between act and sworn statement = consciousness of guilt flag
- Precedent: S v Saoli 2015 (2) SACR 49 (SCA)

### B6 — Financial Brain
- Every transaction reconciled against ledgers and tax codes
- Simulates fraud patterns to identify hidden structures
- Auto-generates tax returns for identified jurisdictions
- Rent tracing capability added in v5.2.8

### B7 — Legal Brain
- Jurisdiction-specific: UAE, South Africa, United States, European Union, United Nations
- Auto-maps statutes, precedents, treaties
- Online retrieval: SAFLII, PACER, BAILII, LexisNexis (v5.2.8)
- Offline mode: static statute database for all mapped jurisdictions

### B8 — Audio Brain
- Whisper.cpp for on-device transcription (no cloud)
- Speaker diarization: who spoke when
- Synthetic audio detection: deepfake identification
- Tamper detection: sample rate inconsistencies, unnatural silence gaps

### B9 — R&D Brain
- **Cannot issue verdicts.** This is constitutional.
- Trains all other brains via simulated evidence
- Adversarial red-team testing: deliberately tries to fool B1-B8
- Validates calibration across all brains
- v5.2.8: may vote ONLY on authentication of online judicial records retrieved by B7
