## 2. The Genesis — Why Templates Exist

**The Problem Templates Solve:**

Before templates, forensic AI suffered from:
- **Inconsistency:** Same evidence, different prompts = different conclusions
- **Hallucination:** AI inventing legal citations, misquoting documents, fabricating precedents
- **Jurisdiction confusion:** Applying UAE law to a South African case
- **No audit trail:** No way to prove how a conclusion was reached
- **Inadmissibility:** Courts rejecting AI-generated analysis without chain of custody

**The Template Solution:**

| Requirement | Template Enforcement |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Consistency | Identical section order, mandatory fields, no skipping |
| Hallucination prevention | SHA-512 verification, source anchoring, closed-fail validation |
| Jurisdiction accuracy | Pre-declared jurisdiction matrix with legal article auto-mapping |
| Audit trail | Every claim token-locked, hashed, blockchain-anchored |
| Court admissibility | PDF/A-3B output, Daubert-compliant methodology, ISO 24027:2021 |
