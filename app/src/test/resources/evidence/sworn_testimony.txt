AFFIDAVIT IN THE CONSTITUTIONAL COURT OF SOUTH AFRICA

Case: CCT237/20
Date: 18 June 2021
Deponent: Director A, AllFuels (Pty) Ltd

I, the undersigned, being duly sworn, do hereby depose and say:

1. I am a director of AllFuels (Pty) Ltd and have personal knowledge of the facts herein.

2. The Memorandum of Understanding referred to by the Applicant was merely a discussion document.

3. There was never any intention to transfer ownership of the Company to the Applicant.

4. The document was never intended to be legally binding.

5. No goodwill was ever transferred, and in any event, the goodwill of the site had no quantifiable value.

6. The Applicant's claim to ownership is based on a misunderstanding of the nature of the document.

7. I verily believe that what is stated above is true.

Sworn to and signed before me on this 18th day of June 2021.

Commissioner of Oaths
